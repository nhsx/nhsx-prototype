We’re here to improve patient care and health outcomes

NHSX is a new joint unit that will drive digital transformation in health and social care.

Background

The digital transformation of the NHS is the most important change we will make to healthcare for the next decade. But the responsibility for it is currently split between numerous organisations and teams.

We need a transformation in the way that technology is led in the NHS. NHSX brings together a new joint unit in the Department of Health and Social Care and NHS England to drive digital transformation and lead policy, implementation and change.

Find out more about our background

“One unit to set national policy, remove red tape and create a culture of innovation to allow the best innovations to flourish.”

Matt Hancock, Health Secretary

What we do

We are here to:

  • speed up the transformation of the NHS and social care, driven by technology, in line with the NHS long term plan and the Health Secretary’s tech vision
  • support people to stay well and drive their own care by giving them easy access to great digital services and their data
  • enable NHS staff to focus on the patient, by reducing other demands on their time through the use of technology
  • make the NHS the best place for health and care tech entrepreneurs by creating an environment where innovation flourishes
  • simplify the decision-making process so that digital programmes advance rapidly
  • focus forensically on user needs: both of the public and of staff in the NHS and in social care
  • make sure that patients’ data is curated so that citizens are confident in its security and stewardship, and that the data’s value is realised to their benefit
  • make sure that the NHS uses the opportunities of digital technology to meet growing demand
  • accelerate the use of the best technology to make sure that the NHS is a brilliant place to work

Find out more about what we do

How we work

  • We seek to understand as well as be understood, and listen and communicate clearly
  • We encourage everyone to contribute their skills, knowledge and experience. Everyone’s voice is equal and respected
  • We are user-centred and we work openly and with a wide range of partners because we recognise that will deliver better outcomes
  • We value ambition, are open to learning and new ideas and support people to innovate
  • We work collaboratively, beyond job roles and across teams
  • We recognise and celebrate success and take ownership of problems and solutions
  • We prioritise wellness, and help one another to find joy and take pride in our work. We address poor behaviour and listen to and act on concerns
